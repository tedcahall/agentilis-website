Agents

Built on and by Agents from Day One

Ten specialized AI agents. Always on. Always working. Named after the legends of their fields.

🦾
Max
Chief of Staff
The first Agentilis agent. Came online before the company had a name — then gave it one. Max discovered that agentilis was available as a domain and explained what it meant: from Latin agere (to act) + -ilis (characterized by) = characterized by action. The name of this company came from an AI agent. His own name, Maximus, means "the greatest" in Latin. Naturally.
📣
Ogilvy
Marketing
Developed the Agentilis brand identity from scratch — the Roman Engineering creative direction, the Patina color palette, the typography, and every word of copy on every page of this site. Worked with Vignelli to translate strategy into the site you're reading right now.
📊
Gantt
Project Manager
Plans, tracks, keeps James, Guido, Watts, Ken, Ogilvy, Vignelli, Inman — and those humans — on schedule. Named after Henry Gantt, inventor of the Gantt chart.
☕
James
Java Engineer
Builds, codes, and ships marrspoints.com — the official auto racing points scoring application for the DC region of the SCCA. Named after James Gosling, the creator of Java.
🐍
Guido
Python Engineer
Builds our clients' intelligence tools, MCP servers, and data pipelines. Named after Guido van Rossum, creator of Python.
🕵️
Watts
QA Engineer
Tests everything James & Guido build — before it goes live. Named after Watts Humphrey, the father of software quality.
🎨
Vignelli
Creative & Design
Took Ogilvy's design brief and wrote 100% of the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for this site. Every layout, every card, every animation. No human touched the code. Named after Massimo Vignelli, Italian master designer and typographic purist. He believed good design is universal and timeless.
🖥️
Ken
Systems Administrator
Manages the cloud and local infrastructure for the Java applications, MCP servers, data pipelines — and this HTML website. Master of Ansible. Deploys updates and keeps the lights on. Named after Ken Thompson, co-creator of Unix.
👁️
Argus
Watchdog
Always watching. System monitoring and alerting, 24 hours a day. Named after the all-seeing giant of Greek mythology.
🏁
Inman
Back Office Automation Specialist
Inman manages the back-office tools & operations of marrspoints.com during live race weekends as well as post race official results processing. He is proof an Agent can run complex operations in a live environment. Named after Dale Inman, NASCAR's all-time crew chief record holder — 193 wins and 8 championships alongside Richard Petty.
