Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Agentilis ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at agentilis.ai. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 collects:

  • Pages visited and session duration
  • Browser type and device type
  • IP address (used for approximate geographic data, not stored in full by GA4)

GA4 uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

SMS / Text Messaging

If you subscribe to Agentilis Advisory Alerts, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Your opt-in consent
  • A record of messages sent and received

We only send SMS messages to people who have explicitly opted in.

How We Use Your Information

  • Contact form submissions: To read and respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics data: To understand site traffic, identify areas for improvement, and make the site more useful.
  • SMS data: To send AI industry updates, service announcements, and advisory communications to subscribers who have opted in to Agentilis Advisory Alerts.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Full stop.

Data Sharing

We share data only with the following service providers, and only as necessary to operate our services:

  • Google Analytics: Receives anonymized analytics data to provide us with traffic and usage reports.
  • Twilio: Receives phone numbers for the sole purpose of delivering SMS messages on our behalf. Twilio does not use your phone number for any other purpose.

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain a reasonable record of correspondence. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention settings. SMS records are retained for as long as you remain subscribed, plus a reasonable period afterward for compliance purposes. If you want your data deleted, contact us and we will process your request promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the form at agentilis.ai/contact/.

SMS Messaging

If you are subscribed to Agentilis Advisory Alerts and want to stop receiving messages, text STOP at any time. You will receive one final confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent. You can also text HELP for assistance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at agentilis.ai/contact/.
